# 小作文模板（石雷鹏体系）

> 小作文 10分 | 常考：投诉信、推荐信、申请信、感谢信、建议信、邀请信、道歉信
> 万能开头：`I am writing to...` | 万能结尾：`I look forward to your reply.`

---

## 投诉信 Letter of Complaint

**开头**
> I am writing to express my dissatisfaction/complaint about **[问题]** that I encountered on **[时间/地点]**.

**主体（三点式）**
> First and foremost, **[具体问题1，越详细越有力]**. Furthermore, **[问题2]**. What makes matters worse is that **[问题3/影响]**.

**结尾**
> I would appreciate it if you could **[具体诉求：退款/道歉/整改]** at your earliest convenience. I look forward to your prompt response.

---

## 推荐信 Letter of Recommendation

**开头**
> It is my great honor/pleasure to recommend **[姓名]** for **[目的：the position/the program/the award]**.

**主体**
> During his/her **[时间]** at **[机构]**, [姓名] has consistently demonstrated exceptional **[品质1]**. For instance, **[具体事例]**. In addition, **[品质2]** is equally remarkable, as evidenced by **[事例]**.

**结尾**
> I am fully confident that **[姓名]** will prove to be a valuable asset. Please feel free to contact me should you require further information.

---

## 申请信 Letter of Application

**开头**
> I am writing to apply for **[职位/项目/资格]** advertised/offered by **[机构]**.

**主体**
> I believe I am a strong candidate for this position for the following reasons. To begin with, I have **[相关经验/技能]**, which has equipped me with **[能力]**. Moreover, my **[品质/成就]** demonstrates my ability to **[胜任说明]**.

**结尾**
> I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my application further. Please find my resume attached for your consideration.

---

## 感谢信 Letter of Thanks

**开头**
> I am writing to extend my heartfelt/sincerest gratitude for **[具体帮助/礼物/机会]**.

**主体**
> Your **[行为]** came at a critical moment when **[背景]**. Thanks to your support, I was able to **[结果]**. This experience has taught me **[体会]**, which I will carry with me throughout my life.

**结尾**
> Your kindness will always be remembered. I hope to have the opportunity to return the favor someday.

---

## 建议信 Letter of Suggestion

**开头**
> I am writing to put forward some suggestions regarding **[主题]**, which I believe will contribute significantly to **[目标]**.

**主体**
> First, I would suggest that **[建议1]**. This would help **[效果]**. Second, it might be worthwhile to consider **[建议2]**, as **[原因]**. Finally, **[建议3]**.

**结尾**
> I sincerely hope these suggestions will be taken into consideration. I am available to discuss them further at your convenience.

---

## 邀请信 Letter of Invitation

**开头**
> On behalf of **[组织]**, I am delighted/honored to invite you to **[活动]** to be held on **[时间]** at **[地点]**.

**主体**
> The event will feature **[内容1]**, along with **[内容2]**. Your participation would be of great significance because **[原因]**.

**结尾**
> We would be honored by your presence. Please confirm your attendance by **[截止日期]**.

## 万能高分句式

| 功能 | 普通写法 | 高分写法 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 表示原因 | because | owing to / in light of / given that |
| 表示结果 | so | consequently / as a result / thereby |
| 转折 | but | however / nevertheless / nonetheless |
| 递进 | also | furthermore / in addition / what's more |
| 强调 | very important | of paramount importance |
| 建议 | should | it would be advisable to / I strongly recommend |

## 词汇替换速查

| 普通 | 高级 |
|------|------|
| help | facilitate / assist / render assistance |
| show | demonstrate / illustrate / manifest |
| think | believe / consider / maintain / hold |
| problem | issue / concern / drawback / shortcoming |
| important | crucial / vital / indispensable / imperative |
| good | exceptional / outstanding / commendable |
| bad | unsatisfactory / inadequate / detrimental |
