# 小作文模板（石雷鹏体系）

> 小作文 10分，常见题型：投诉信、推荐信、申请信、感谢信、建议信

## 通用结构

```
第一段（开头）：说明写作目的
第二段（主体）：具体说明/建议/申请内容（2-3点）
第三段（结尾）：期待回复/表达感谢/重申请求
```

## 投诉信 (Letter of Complaint)

**开头：**
> I am writing to express my dissatisfaction/complaint about...

**主体（举例模板）：**
> First and foremost, ... Furthermore, ... Last but not least, ...

**结尾：**
> I would appreciate it if you could... I look forward to your prompt response.

## 推荐信 (Letter of Recommendation)

**开头：**
> It is my great pleasure to recommend [Name] for...

**主体：**
> [Name] has demonstrated exceptional [quality] in... For instance, ...

**结尾：**
> I am confident that [Name] will prove to be a valuable asset to...

## 申请信 (Letter of Application)

**开头：**
> I am writing to apply for the position of... / I am interested in...

**主体：**
> I have [experience/qualification]... My [skill] will enable me to...

**结尾：**
> I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my application further.

## 感谢信 (Letter of Thanks)

**开头：**
> I am writing to extend my sincere gratitude for...

**结尾：**
> Your kindness will always be remembered.

## 高分词汇替换

| 普通 | 高级 |
|------|------|
| very good | exceptional/outstanding |
| help | facilitate/assist |
| show | demonstrate/illustrate |
| think | believe/consider/maintain |
